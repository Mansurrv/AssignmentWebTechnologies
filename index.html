Privacy & Data Policy

1. Data Collection

We collect audio, video, and screen-sharing data solely for the purpose of maintaining exam integrity. Data collection is strictly limited to the duration of the proctored session and is subject to the terms agreed upon by the institution and the student.

2. Data Storage and Security

All collected data is encrypted using AES-256 and stored on secure, geographically-localized servers. Access is restricted to authorized proctors and administrators. We adhere to GDPR and FERPA compliance guidelines.

3. Data Retention and Destruction

Unless legally required otherwise, all proctoring session data is automatically purged from our servers 90 days after the final grade certification period. Users can request manual data deletion via the Contact page.

By using SecureExam, you consent to the terms outlined in this policy. Failure to consent will prevent participation in proctored examinations.
